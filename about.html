    Our Story

    Yuvraj, means I was working in Pocket Café at Pimpri Chinchawad, Pune. Founded on 10/10/2021 by Yuvraj Ithape , Backyard café is a online snacks ordering and Booking a place for celebrating your special day in café in thousand of cities in Maharashtra. Backyard Provides digital menu on its website from which customers can securely pay with the most options available out of all cafe e-commerce systems. A customer can book a café for the celebration. Backyard Café also provide decoration Packages where customer can book their package for decoration of café on their special day and can celebrate with full joy.
